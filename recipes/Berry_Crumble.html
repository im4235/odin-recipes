Description

This berry crumble recipe may berry well be my favorite summer dessert, with the perfect balance of sweet to tart, tender to crisp, and fruit to crumble.

Ingredients

    Crumble
    • Flour
    • Sugar
    • Baking powder
    • Salt
    • Unsalted butter
    • Egg yolk
    • Vanilla extract
    • Apple cider Vinegar
    Filling
    • Berries
    • Sugar
    • Cornstarch
    • Lemon
    • Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Make crumble mixture
  3. Stir in egg yolks and vanilla with a fork until egg is mostly absorbed
  4. Add 1 teaspoon vinegar; mix briefly
  5. Make filling
  6. Assemble crumble
  7. Bake until top is beautifully browned and berry filling bubbles, 40 to 45 minutes
  8. Let cool to room temperature before serving, about 30 minutes
