Odin Recipes

Description

It's beerita time! Who would believe that beer would be the perfect solution to eradicating fluorescent green margaritas?

Ingredients

  • frozen limeade concentrate
  • tequila
  • water
  • beer
  • ice
  • lime

Steps

  1. Pour limeade concentrate, tequila, water, and beer into a large pitcher. Stir until limeade has melted
  2. Add plenty of ice to the pitcher and garnish with lime wedges
